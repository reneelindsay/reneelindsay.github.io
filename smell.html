Smell accounts for a large portion of our sense of taste, and nowhere is that more apparent than in our beloved coffee bean. Professional coffee tasters are like sommiliers; they must be mindful of each bean’s growing environment and the strength of roast in order to catch flavor notes such as black pepper, honey, cinnamon, caramel, malt, and florals. Beans differ in balance, acidity and bitterness.

Advanced tasters can even predict upcoming weather patterns in the coffee bean’s country of origin. Including tornadoes. Ooo.

Scent triggers memory, which accounts for much of the “comfort” in our favorite foods. Combined with the pep coffee gives us, and the soothing rhythm of ritual, morning coffee brings a sense of delight and grounding through our senses, mostly notably our sense of smell. Breathe in and enjoy.
