Starlight casts its silver sheen across your comforter, warm from the heat of your slumbering form.

You hear a light scratching noise at your bedroom door, followed by a tentative meow. You ignore it and roll over, hiding underneath the covers. A moment later, the alarm on your phone begins to chime. 5:30 am.

You groan and stretch, reach a hand out from underneath your blankets to stop the noise. A few deep breaths, and another meow from behind your door.

The cat is up and ready for breakfast. You roll out of bed, find your glasses, put on some socks, blink a few times, and get ready to face the day.

This little site celebrates all things coffee, particularly of the morning kind.

Each page visits a part of the process of making morning coffee, with an evolving design reflective of the shift from night to day, from sleep to wakefulness, a story for the senses.

For added fun, browse the source code for coffee commentary within the markup itself. Call it a playful exercise in crafting artistic code.
